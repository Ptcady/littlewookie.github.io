Who We Are

Mercy General Hospital provides vastly superior medical care in a comforting environment with the best benefits and innovations in medical technology and family friendly design.

As the largest medical center in the state of Arizona, we at Mercy General strive to offer the best in medical expertise.

Cardiatric Research

John Hopkins Research Institute, affiliated with Mercy General, is devoted to the understanding, preventation and investigation of cardiatric illnessess and injuries. All cardiatric research at Mercy General is conducted through the John Hopkins Research Institute. It has been designated a John Hopkins University research center for its central role in the university's integrated research program.

Founded in 1982, our cardiatric research institute has more than 200 investigators, 500 staff members and 100 trainees who contribute to six growing programs in basic research and translations medicine at the research facility. We also have interdisciplinary centers of excellence target specific health issues and complement our core programs.

Foundation

Mercy General relies on philanthropic funding to enhance its programs and services for families. Founded in 1984, the Mercy General Hospital Foundation is a charitable 501(c)(3) tax-exempt origanization with fundraising professionals who work with 11 affiliated organizations and an active team of volunteers to cultivate and secure contributions that advance the mission of the hospital.
